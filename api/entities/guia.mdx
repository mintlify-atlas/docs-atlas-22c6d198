## Overview

The `GuiayOrdendeCompra` entity represents delivery guides and purchase orders in the system. It tracks purchase information including supplier, date, item description, quantity, and pricing.

**Package:** `com.bienes.entidad`

**Imports:**
- `java.sql.Date`
